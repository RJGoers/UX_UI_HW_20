Case Study Overview

THE PROBLEM: The solo traveler needs to feel safe and secure to feel more confident and comfortable in order to travel more often. THE SOLUTION: I will design using the 4 phases of the UX design process; Discover, Define, Develop, and Deliver. I will use a human-centered design approach to empathize with the needs of solo travelers. MY ROLE: UX designer (Individual Project) TOOLS: Miro, Figma, Storyboard.net, Google Drive, Otter.ai, Zoom.

PROCESS & TIMELINE: This was a 4 week sprint with each phase lasting 1 week.
